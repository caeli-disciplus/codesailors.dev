Insights and advice for developers at all stages of their careers.

## Junior Developer: Learn the Fundamentals

Resist the urge to jump to the newest framework. Deep knowledge of how the web actually works — HTTP, the DOM, the event loop — compounds over your entire career.

## Mid-level: Own a Domain

Pick an area and go deep. Being the person who truly understands performance, accessibility, or system design on your team is worth more than being average at everything.

## Salary Negotiation

Always negotiate. The first offer is rarely the best offer. Research market rates on levels.fyi and Glassdoor, and let the employer anchor to a number first.

## Building in Public

Writing, speaking, and contributing to open source creates serendipitous career opportunities that job boards never will. Start small — a technical post a month is enough.
